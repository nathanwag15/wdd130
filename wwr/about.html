At Down the River Rafting, our mission is to provide thrilling and safe rafting adventures, connecting people with nature's beauty. Through expert guidance and environmental responsibility, we create lasting memories that inspire camaraderie and respect for our planet's waterways."

History

Established in 1995, Down the River Rafting emerged from a group of thrill-seekers exploring Rapids River. Pioneering cutting-edge rafting tech in 2003, we've since become synonymous with safe, exhilarating adventures. Committed to conservation, our legacy blends expertise, adventure, and eco-consciousness. Join us for a history-making ride down the rivers we've conquered and cherished.
