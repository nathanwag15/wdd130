Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to give our customers the information they need in order to have a great adventure rafting and to be properly prepared before meeting with our skilled workers.

Audience

Young Adults who are looking to have an adventure rafting but might not have the equipment or the skills needed to go on their own. They expect to find information to help them feel comfortable with rafting and confidence that we have the skills to give them a great experience. They will access this through desktop.
